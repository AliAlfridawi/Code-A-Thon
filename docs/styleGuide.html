Accessibility Guidance

  • Keep contrast strong: body text should remain above WCAG AA contrast targets against both light and dark backgrounds.
  • Do not remove focus styles: use clear ring indicators for keyboard and assistive technology users.
  • Use semantic labels and concise helper text in forms to reduce confusion during mentor-mentee onboarding.
  • Avoid color-only status messaging: pair badges with text labels like "On Track" or "Needs Follow-Up".
